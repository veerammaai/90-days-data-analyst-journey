---

## 🎯 Business Questions Answered

### 1. Which orders resulted in zero or negative profit?

* **Filter Applied:** Profit ≤ 0
* **Insight:** Office Supplies had the highest number of loss-making orders, while Furniture recorded the highest total loss (~$61K). Technology had the least losses (~13%).

---

### 2. Which Technology product generated the highest sales?

* **Insight:** The *Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier* was the highest-selling product in the Technology category.

---

### 3. Which region appears the most in Consumer segment orders?

* **Insight:** The West region had the highest number of Consumer segment orders, followed by the East region.

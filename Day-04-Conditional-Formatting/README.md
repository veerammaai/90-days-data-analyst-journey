# Day 4 – Conditional Formatting in Excel

## 📌 Objective

The objective of Day 4 was to learn and practice **Conditional Formatting** in Microsoft Excel to improve data visualization and quickly identify patterns, trends, and exceptions in a sales dataset.

---

## 📚 Topics Covered

* Highlight Cells Rules
* Greater Than
* Less Than
* Between
* Top 10 Items
* Data Bars
* Color Scales
* Icon Sets


---

## 🛠️ Practice

Applied Conditional Formatting on a retail sales dataset to:

* Highlight sales greater than **500** (Green)
* Highlight sales between **300–500** (Yellow)
* Highlight sales below **300** (Red)
* Highlight positive and negative profit values
* Highlight the Top 10 sales records
  

---

## 🎯 Key Takeaways

* Learned how Conditional Formatting improves data readability.
* Used color coding to quickly identify high and low-performing records.
* Explored different formatting options available in Excel.
* Understood how visual formatting supports faster business analysis.
